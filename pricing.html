Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the emissions data?

Our data is based on aircraft type, age, and publicly available fuel efficiency research. We use ICAO methodology as our baseline and layer in aircraft-specific data for greater accuracy.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from your account dashboard with no cancellation fees.

What format are the PDF reports?

Reports are generated as clean, branded PDFs that include route comparisons, annual CO₂ savings, real-world equivalents, and a full airline breakdown — ready to share with your sustainability team.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes — we offer 50% off Pro plans for registered nonprofits and educational institutions. Contact us through the waitlist form and mention your organization.

How does the API work?

Enterprise customers get access to our REST API, which allows booking engines and travel management platforms to query emissions data in real time and surface green options directly in their UI.

Is my company's travel data private?

Absolutely. Enterprise travel history data is encrypted, never shared with third parties, and deleted upon request. We take data privacy seriously.
